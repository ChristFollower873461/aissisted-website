Support

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Use this page for help with Grail Fit Check, workspace access, proof requests, connected channels, subscriptions, and account or deletion requests.

Contact support: Email pj@aissistedconsulting.com. Include your business name, website, the email used for Grail, and a short description of what you need.

Typical response time: 1 to 2 business days.

Workspace Access

The Grail iOS app is free to download, but the operational workspace requires an active Grail workspace. If the app says no active workspace was found, complete the Fit Check or contact support with your business details.

Fit Check Help

The Fit Check asks for business facts, service area, channels, proof supply, cadence, publishing comfort, and contact details. It helps AIssisted Consulting prepare the first workspace packet. Avoid sending private customer secrets, regulated records, or third-party confidential material through Fit Check.

Proof And Drafts

Grail drafts from approved business facts and customer-safe proof. If a draft is blocked, it usually means the update needs stronger proof, source detail, owner approval, or a safer claim.

Connected Channels

Grail may prepare work for Google Business Profile, social scheduling, social profiles, website/SEO, and reporting workflows when the customer has authorized the relevant access. Channel availability depends on plan, proof supply, account access, and platform rules.

Subscription Or Billing Help

If you joined Grail through a web checkout, use the billing portal or support email from your receipt. If a future iOS subscription is offered through Apple, manage it through Apple ID settings. Email pj@aissistedconsulting.com if you are unsure which path applies.

Account Or Data Deletion

To request workspace closure, account deletion, or deletion of submitted Fit Check/support data, email pj@aissistedconsulting.com. Some records may need to be retained where required for billing, legal, security, or audit reasons.

Privacy And Terms

Review the Grail Privacy Policy and Grail Terms for more details.
