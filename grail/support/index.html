Campaigns And Proof

How do I build a campaign pack?

Open Command or Drafts and choose Build campaign pack. Add your business, offer, market, approved proof fact, public source URL, call to action, and channels. Grail creates a separate draft for each selected channel.

Why did Proof Lock fail?

Proof Lock fails when required business, offer, market, or proof language is missing from a draft. Open the draft to see the missing anchors, restore or revise the supported language, and choose Save + recheck.

What counts as a public source?

Use a real HTTPS page that a customer or reviewer can open without credentials. The page should support the factual claim used in the campaign. Grail blocks local, test, and placeholder addresses.

Workspace And Review

Does Grail publish automatically?

No. The mobile workspace builds, edits, proof-checks, copies, and queues drafts for owner review. Publishing, scheduling, messages, advertising spend, and channel connections require separate account authority.

Where is my work saved?

Campaign drafts, proof packets, owner-review settings, and recent activity are saved on the device. Removing the app also removes that local workspace data.

Why can’t I move a draft into owner review?

  1. Confirm the owner-review queue is enabled in Settings.
  2. Open the draft and make sure Proof Lock passes.
  3. Choose Move to owner review.

Troubleshooting

A source page will not open

Check your connection and confirm that the URL begins with HTTPS. If the page has moved or requires a login, replace it with a stable public source before using the draft.

My saved workspace did not load

Close and reopen Grail. If the problem continues, include your device model, iOS version, and the action you were taking when you contact support. Do not include private customer information.
