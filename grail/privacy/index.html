Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Grail is provided by AIssisted Consulting LLC. This policy explains how Grail handles information submitted through the Grail website, Fit Check, mobile app, onboarding, support, and active workspace services.

Summary: Grail uses business-provided facts, proof, channel preferences, and support details to build and manage a source-backed marketing workspace. Grail is not designed to sell personal information, run advertising trackers, or publish customer material without workspace authority.

Information Grail May Handle

Depending on how you use Grail, AIssisted Consulting may receive or process:

  • Business name, website, service area, business type, services, offers, and public business details.
  • Contact details such as name, email address, phone number, and support messages.
  • Fit Check answers about channels, proof supply, cadence, publishing comfort, and marketing pains.
  • Customer-safe proof such as reviews, review themes, service notes, FAQs, offers, source URLs, photos, case studies, or other material you choose to provide.
  • Workspace status, draft status, proof gaps, source anchors, connector readiness, and owner authority rules.
  • Technical information needed to run, secure, diagnose, and improve the app, website, or workspace.

How We Use Information

We use Grail information to prepare Fit Check packets, create and operate workspaces, draft source-backed marketing updates, request missing proof, provide support, maintain records of approval and authority, prevent duplicate or unsupported copy, and improve reliability.

Marketing Channels And Connected Services

If a customer connects or authorizes marketing channels, Grail may use approved information to prepare channel-specific work for surfaces such as Google Business Profile, social schedulers, social profiles, website/SEO workflows, or reporting tools. Channel access is controlled by the workspace authority rules selected for the customer.

Grail does not promise that every draft will be published. Proof Lock may block thin, duplicate, risky, or unsupported updates.

Payments

Grail subscriptions and setup payments may be handled by third-party payment providers such as Stripe or platform stores. AIssisted Consulting does not store full payment card numbers. Payment providers process payment information under their own policies.

Sharing

We may share information with service providers needed to operate Grail, such as hosting, email, payment, scheduling, analytics, app distribution, support, and connected-channel providers. We do not sell personal information.

Retention And Deletion

We keep workspace, support, billing, and proof records as long as needed to operate Grail, meet legal or accounting obligations, preserve approval records, and support customers. To request deletion or account/workspace closure, email pj@aissistedconsulting.com. Some records may need to be retained where required for legal, billing, security, or audit reasons.

Children

Grail is intended for business owners and operators. It is not directed to children.

Security

We use reasonable safeguards to protect information handled through Grail. No system is perfectly secure, and customers should avoid submitting private secrets, regulated customer records, or confidential third-party information unless a specific secure process has been agreed to.

Changes

We may update this policy as Grail changes. The latest version will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, email pj@aissistedconsulting.com or use the AIssisted Consulting contact page.
