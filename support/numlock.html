How to Play

Gameplay FAQ

NumLock is a daily number puzzle game. Here's how it works:

  1. The Goal: Guess the secret 5-digit number in 6 tries or fewer
  2. Make a Guess: Enter any 5-digit number (digits can repeat)
  3. Get Feedback: After each guess, you'll see color-coded hints:
1
2
3
4
5
  • Green: Correct digit in the correct position
  • Yellow/Gold: Correct digit in the wrong position
  • Gray: Digit not in the secret number

Use the feedback to narrow down the answer and solve the puzzle!

Your score is based on several factors:

  • Speed: Faster solves earn more points
  • Attempts: Solving in fewer guesses gives bonus points
  • Streaks: Consecutive daily solves multiply your score
  • Perfect Solve: Guess correctly on the first try for maximum points
💡 Scoring Tip

Maintain your daily streak! Each consecutive day you solve the puzzle, your streak multiplier increases, dramatically boosting your score.

Hints help you when you're stuck:

  • Free Users: Get 1 hint per puzzle
  • Premium Users: Get unlimited hints

Types of Hints:

  • Reveal Digit: Shows one correct digit in its position
  • Eliminate: Removes one incorrect digit from the keyboard
  • Position Clue: Tells you if a specific digit is in the right spot

Tap the lightbulb icon (💡) during gameplay to use a hint.

Every day at midnight local time, a new puzzle is released:

  • All players worldwide get the same puzzle each day
  • You have 24 hours to solve it before the next puzzle appears
  • Your solve time and attempts are recorded for the leaderboard
  • Missing a day breaks your streak

Practice Mode: Premium users can play unlimited practice puzzles anytime without affecting their daily streak.

Subscriptions

Premium Subscription FAQ

NumLock Premium unlocks the full game experience:

  • Unlimited Hints: Never get stuck again
  • Practice Mode: Play unlimited puzzles anytime
  • Puzzle Archive: Access all past daily puzzles
  • Detailed Stats: Track your progress with advanced analytics
  • Custom Themes: Unlock exclusive color themes
  • No Ads: Enjoy uninterrupted gameplay
  • Leaderboard Access: See global rankings and compete

NumLock Premium offers flexible pricing:

  • Monthly: $2.99/month
  • Yearly: $19.99/year (save 44%)

Prices may vary by region. All subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

All subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top (Apple ID)
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find and tap NumLock
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription or change your plan
💡 Important

Deleting the app does not cancel your subscription. You must cancel through Settings → Subscriptions to stop billing.

If you've previously purchased Premium and it's not showing:

  1. Open NumLock
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the top right
  3. Tap Restore Purchases
  4. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase if prompted

Your Premium features should restore immediately. If not, try force-quitting and reopening the app.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

If your game progress or streak isn't being saved:

  1. Ensure you're connected to the internet when completing puzzles
  2. Check that you're signed in with the same Apple ID across devices
  3. Enable iCloud if you want progress synced across devices
  4. Try force-quitting the app and reopening it
⚠️ Note

Progress is tied to your device unless you're signed in and have iCloud enabled. If you delete the app without these enabled, your progress may be lost.

If your scores aren't appearing on the leaderboard:

  1. Ensure you have an internet connection when completing puzzles
  2. Check that Leaderboards are enabled in Settings → Privacy & Security → Game Center
  3. Sign in to Game Center if prompted
  4. Scores only upload after you complete or give up on a puzzle

Leaderboard updates may take a few minutes to appear. If issues persist, try logging out and back into Game Center.

New puzzles release at midnight in your local time zone. If you don't see a new puzzle:

  1. Pull down on the main screen to refresh
  2. Ensure you have an internet connection
  3. Close and reopen the app
  4. Check that your device's time and date are set correctly (Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically)

If you still see yesterday's puzzle after trying these steps, contact support.

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Force-quit the app: Swipe up from bottom (or double-tap home), swipe up on NumLock
  2. Update to the latest version from the App Store
  3. Restart your device
  4. Free up storage space on your device (Settings → General → iPhone Storage)
  5. Delete and reinstall the app (your progress will restore if synced)

If crashes continue, please contact support with your device model and iOS version.

If you're having trouble with hints:

  • Free users: You get only 1 hint per puzzle. Once used, hints are unavailable until the next daily puzzle.
  • Premium users: Ensure your subscription is active. Check in Settings → Subscription Status.
  • Try force-quitting and reopening the app
  • Restore purchases if Premium features aren't showing
