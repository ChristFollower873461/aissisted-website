Using PestFriend

Frequently Asked Questions

PestFriend uses advanced AI to identify pests from your photos:

  1. Take a Photo: Snap a clear picture of the pest or evidence (droppings, damage, etc.)
  2. Upload: The image is securely sent to our AI for analysis
  3. Get Results: Within seconds, you'll receive the pest identification with confidence level
  4. Learn More: Tap the result to view detailed information about the pest
💡 Photo Tips

For best results, ensure good lighting, get close to the pest, and keep the camera steady. Multiple angles help with accurate identification.

The AI chat assistant is your personal pest control expert. Ask it about:

  • Prevention Tips: How to keep specific pests away
  • Identification Help: "What does a bed bug look like?"
  • Treatment Advice: "How do I get rid of ants in my kitchen?"
  • Safety Information: "Are these spiders dangerous?"
  • Product Recommendations: "What spray works for roaches?"
  • Follow-up Questions: Deep dive into any pest topic
🔒 Privacy Note

Chat conversations are processed by AI to provide answers but are not stored permanently or used to train models.

Once a pest is identified, PestFriend provides comprehensive treatment guides:

  • DIY Methods: Step-by-step home remedies and prevention tips
  • Product Recommendations: Suggested treatments with safety information
  • Safety Warnings: Important precautions for handling chemicals or infestations
  • When to Call a Pro: Guidance on when professional help is recommended
  • Prevention: Long-term strategies to keep pests from returning

Pro Users get access to advanced treatment protocols and priority recommendations.

Privacy

Your Privacy Matters

We take your privacy seriously:

  • Processing: Photos are sent to our AI service solely for identification
  • No Permanent Storage: Images are not stored permanently on our servers
  • No Training: Your photos are never used to train AI models
  • Secure Transmission: All uploads use encrypted connections
  • Local Storage: Photos are stored on your device only (unless you delete them)
🔒 Our Promise

We don't sell, share, or retain your pest photos beyond the immediate identification process. Your privacy is our priority.

Chat conversations have limited retention:

  • Temporary Processing: Chats are processed in real-time to generate responses
  • Session-Based: Conversations are stored temporarily during your session
  • No Training Use: Chats are not used to train or improve AI models
  • Clear History: You can clear chat history anytime in Settings

PestFriend collects minimal data:

  • App Usage: Basic analytics to improve the app (optional, can be disabled)
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous data to fix bugs and crashes
  • Purchase History: To validate subscriptions and restore purchases

We do not collect personal information, location data (unless you grant permission), or pest photo content for any purpose other than identification.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

If you're having camera issues:

Enable Camera Permissions

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Scroll down and tap PestFriend
  3. Tap Camera
  4. Select Allow

Additional troubleshooting:

  • Force-quit the app and reopen it
  • Restart your device
  • Ensure you have available storage space
  • Update to the latest iOS version

If the AI identification or chat isn't working:

  1. Check your internet connection (WiFi or cellular)
  2. Try switching between WiFi and cellular data
  3. Close and reopen the app
  4. Check if there's a service outage on our status page

Pro Users: You get priority processing. If you're experiencing slowness with an active Pro subscription, please contact support.

If the AI identification doesn't match what you see:

  • Retake the photo: Ensure good lighting and focus
  • Multiple angles: Try photos from different perspectives
  • Include scale: Add a common object for size reference
  • Check confidence score: Low confidence may indicate an unclear image

You can also use the AI Chat to describe what you see for additional help with identification.

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Force-quit the app: Swipe up from bottom (or double-tap home), swipe up on PestFriend
  2. Update to the latest version from the App Store
  3. Restart your device
  4. Free up storage space on your device
  5. Delete and reinstall the app

If crashes continue, please contact support with your device model, iOS version, and what you were doing when the crash occurred.

If your saved identifications are missing:

  1. Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID
  2. Check that you have an active Pro subscription (history is a Pro feature)
  3. Pull down on the History screen to refresh
  4. Check your internet connection

Free users have limited history retention. Upgrade to Pro for unlimited saved identifications.
