Getting Started

What is Wellspring?

Wellspring is a Christian dating app designed for believers who are serious about faith and finding meaningful relationships. We focus on faith stories, values, and genuine connection over superficial swiping.

How do I create a profile?

When you first open Wellspring, you'll go through a 5-step onboarding process: basic info, photos, faith background (denomination and church attendance), your top 3 values, and a short bio with your faith story. Take your time — your profile is how others get to know the real you.

Is Wellspring only for certain denominations?

No! Wellspring welcomes all Christian denominations. You can select your denomination during onboarding, and we use this to help suggest compatible matches. Whether you're Baptist, Catholic, Non-Denominational, or "Just Christian" — you belong here.

Discovery and Matching

How does the Discover feature work?

Discover shows you profiles one at a time as cards. Each card shows the person's photo, name, age, distance, denomination, faith story, and values. You can Pass, Pray, or Like each profile.

What does the Pray button do?

The Pray button is unique to Wellspring. When you tap it, you send an anonymous prayer to that person. It's a way to say "I noticed you and I'm lifting you up" before you ever start a conversation. It's faith in action, even in dating.

How do matches work?

When two people Like each other, it's a match! You'll both appear in each other's Matches tab and can start a conversation. An icebreaker message is required to kick things off — no empty "hey" messages here.

Verification and Trust

How does photo verification work?

To earn your Verified badge, you'll take a selfie matching a specific pose. This confirms you're a real person and that your photos are genuinely you. Verification is required to start matching.

What is the Church Verified badge?

The Church Verified badge (shown as a church icon on your profile) means someone from your church community has vouched for you, or your church leader has provided a verification code. It's optional but highly desirable — it builds trust organically.

Wellspring+ Subscription

What does Wellspring+ include?

Wellspring+ unlocks: unlimited matches, see who liked you, advanced faith filters, priority placement in discovery, and read receipts on messages.

How much does Wellspring+ cost?

Wellspring+ is available as a monthly plan ($9.99/month) or an annual plan ($59.99/year — that's just $5/month, a 50% savings).

How do I manage or cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions on your iPhone, find Wellspring, and you can change or cancel your plan there. You'll continue to have access through the end of your current billing period.

Privacy and Safety

How does Wellspring protect my privacy?

Your data stays on your device as much as possible. We don't sell your information to third parties. Your faith story and personal details are only visible to other verified Wellspring users.

How do I report or block someone?

You can report or block any user from their profile or from a conversation. Reports are reviewed by our team. We take safety seriously — faith-inconsistent behavior and harassment result in immediate action.

What age group is Wellspring for?

Wellspring is for adults 18 and older. The app is rated 17+ due to dating content.
