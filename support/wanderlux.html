Getting Started

Frequently Asked Questions

Searching for your perfect resort is easy:

  1. Tap the Search tab at the bottom of the screen
  2. Enter a destination, resort name, or keyword in the search bar
  3. Browse the results or apply filters to narrow down your options
  4. Tap on any resort to view detailed information, photos, and amenities
💡 Pro Tip

Use natural language searches like "beachfront resorts in Maui" or "all-inclusive Caribbean" for better results.

Wanderlux offers powerful filtering to help you find exactly what you're looking for:

  • Price Range: Set minimum and maximum nightly rates
  • Star Rating: Filter by 3, 4, or 5-star properties
  • Amenities: Select must-have features like pools, spas, beach access, or all-inclusive
  • Property Type: Choose from hotels, resorts, villas, or boutique properties
  • Guest Rating: Filter by traveler review scores

Tap "Filters" on the search screen, select your preferences, then tap "Apply Filters" to see your refined results.

Save your favorite resorts to your personal collection:

  1. Find a resort you love
  2. Tap the bookmark icon (🔖) on the resort card or detail page
  3. The resort is saved to your Bookmarks tab
  4. Access all saved resorts anytime from the Bookmarks tab

With Premium: Create unlimited custom collections (like "Honeymoon Ideas" or "Family Vacations") to organize your bookmarks.

Yes! You can share any resort:

  1. Open the resort detail page
  2. Tap the Share button
  3. Choose your preferred method: Messages, Email, AirDrop, or social media

The share includes resort details, photos, and a link to view it in Wanderlux.

Resort information is updated regularly from our travel partners. Pricing and availability are refreshed daily, while amenities, photos, and descriptions are reviewed weekly. Some data may vary based on what our partners provide.

💡 Note

Always verify pricing and availability directly with the resort or booking site before making reservations.

Subscriptions

Premium Subscription FAQ

Wanderlux Premium unlocks powerful features for serious travelers:

  • Unlimited Bookmarks: Save as many resorts as you want
  • Custom Collections: Organize bookmarks into themed lists
  • Advanced Filters: Access exclusive filters like "Adults Only" and "Pet Friendly"
  • Price Alerts: Get notified when prices drop on saved resorts
  • Offline Access: View saved resorts without internet
  • No Ads: Enjoy an ad-free experience

Wanderlux Premium offers flexible pricing:

  • Monthly: $4.99/month
  • Yearly: $39.99/year (save 33%)

Prices may vary by region. All subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

All subscriptions are managed through your Apple ID:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top (Apple ID)
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find and tap Wanderlux
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription or change your plan
💡 Important

Deleting the app does not cancel your subscription. You must cancel through Settings → Subscriptions to stop billing.

If you've previously purchased Premium and it's not showing:

  1. Open Wanderlux
  2. Go to the Profile tab
  3. Tap Settings (gear icon)
  4. Tap Restore Purchases
  5. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase if prompted

Your Premium features should restore immediately. If not, try force-quitting and reopening the app.

Yes! Wanderlux Premium supports Family Sharing. If you have an active subscription and Family Sharing set up through your Apple ID, up to 5 family members can access Premium features at no additional cost.

To set up Family Sharing: Settings → [Your Name] → Family Sharing → Set Up Your Family.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

Wanderlux needs location permissions to show nearby resorts and provide location-based features:

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Scroll down and tap Wanderlux
  3. Tap Location
  4. Select While Using the App or Always
⚠️ Privacy Note

Your location is only used to show nearby resorts and is never stored or shared with third parties.

If you're having trouble loading resort information:

  1. Check your internet connection
  2. Try force-quitting the app and reopening it
  3. Ensure you have the latest version of Wanderlux from the App Store
  4. Restart your device if problems persist

For Premium offline access: Make sure you've opened and viewed resorts while online before going offline. Bookmarked resorts are automatically cached for offline viewing.

If your bookmarks are missing:

  1. Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID
  2. Check that you're looking at the correct collection (if using Premium)
  3. Pull down on the Bookmarks screen to refresh
  4. If you had Premium but it lapsed, bookmarks may be limited to the free tier maximum

If issues persist, contact support and we can help restore your data.

Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Force-quit the app: Swipe up from bottom (or double-tap home), swipe up on Wanderlux
  2. Update to the latest version from the App Store
  3. Restart your device
  4. Delete and reinstall the app (your bookmarks will restore if you have an account/cloud sync enabled)

If crashes continue, please contact support with your device model and iOS version.

Resort data comes from our travel partners and is refreshed regularly. If you notice outdated information:

  • Pull down on the search results to refresh
  • Try clearing the search and searching again
  • Check your internet connection

For persistent issues with specific resorts, please report them through the app (tap the flag icon on the resort page) or contact support.
