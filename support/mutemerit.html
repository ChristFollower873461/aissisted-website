Getting Started

How MuteMerit Works

MuteMerit helps you stay off your phone and focus on what matters:

  1. Set Your Focus Time: Choose how long you want to stay focused — from 15 minutes to several hours
  2. Pick a Soundscape: Select from calming ambient sounds like rain, forest, ocean waves, white noise, and more
  3. Start Your Session: Put your phone face-down or simply keep it untouched
  4. Earn Merit Points: Complete your session without checking your phone to earn points and badges
💡 Pro Tip

Enable "Strict Mode" for extra motivation — if you pick up your phone before the timer ends, your session is lost!

Merit Points reward you for staying focused:

  • Earning Points: Gain points for every minute of completed focus time
  • Streak Bonuses: Earn multipliers for consecutive days of focus sessions
  • Achievement Badges: Unlock badges for milestones like:
    • 7-Day Streak 🏆
    • 100 Hours Focused ⭐
    • Early Bird (focus before 7 AM) 🌅
    • Night Owl (focus after 9 PM) 🌙
    • Weekend Warrior 🎯

View your stats and achievements in the Profile tab anytime!

Premium Subscription FAQ

MuteMerit Premium unlocks the full focus experience:

  • Exclusive Soundscapes: Access to premium ambient sounds and custom mixes
  • Advanced Stats: Detailed analytics on your focus patterns and trends
  • Custom Themes: Dark mode variants and color customization

MuteMerit Premium subscription details:

  • Monthly: $2.99/month
  • 7-Day Free Trial: Try all Premium features free for one week

Prices may vary by region. All subscriptions auto-renew unless canceled at least 24 hours before the renewal date.

You can cancel your MuteMerit Premium subscription anytime through the App Store:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top (Apple ID)
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Find and tap MuteMerit
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription
⚠️ Important

Deleting the MuteMerit app does NOT cancel your subscription. You must cancel through Settings → Subscriptions to stop future billing. You'll continue to have Premium access until the end of your current billing period.

If you've previously subscribed to Premium and it's not showing:

  1. Open MuteMerit
  2. Tap the Settings icon (gear) in the top right
  3. Tap Restore Purchases
  4. Sign in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase if prompted

Your Premium subscription should restore immediately. If not, try force-quitting and reopening the app.

Privacy & Data

Your Data Privacy

We believe in privacy-first design. MuteMerit collects minimal data:

  • Focus Session Data: Duration and completion status of your focus sessions (stored locally on your device)
  • App Preferences: Your soundscape choices and settings
  • Anonymous Analytics: Optional crash reports and usage patterns (no personal information)

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal identifiers
  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Photos or media
  • Browsing history

Your privacy is our priority:

  • Local-First Storage: Most data stays on your device, not our servers
  • iCloud Sync: Optional iCloud backup encrypted with your Apple ID
  • No Third-Party Sharing: We never sell or share your data with advertisers
  • Family Group Data: Only aggregated focus time is shared; individual session details remain private

You can delete your MuteMerit data at any time:

  1. Open MuteMerit
  2. Go to Settings → Privacy & Data
  3. Tap Clear All Data
  4. Confirm the deletion

This will erase all your focus history, badges, and stats from your device. If you have iCloud sync enabled, this will also remove data from iCloud.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

This is usually a settings issue. Here's how to fix it:

  1. Go to iPhone Settings → MuteMerit
  2. Enable Background App Refresh
  3. Make sure Low Power Mode is off during focus sessions
  4. Keep the app open or ensure it's in your recent apps
💡 Tip

For best results, keep MuteMerit open with your phone face-down. The app is designed to detect when you pick up your phone!

If your streak appears incorrect:

  1. Check that your device's date and time are set correctly (Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically)
  2. Ensure you're connected to the internet for streak synchronization
  3. Sign in with the same Apple ID across devices
  4. Contact support if you believe there's an error

Note: You must complete at least one focus session per day to maintain your streak.

If you can't hear the ambient sounds:

  1. Check that your device volume is up and not muted
  2. Make sure the silent/ringer switch is OFF (orange not showing)
  3. Disconnect any Bluetooth headphones if you want phone speaker audio
  4. Try a different soundscape to see if it's specific to one
  5. Force-quit and reopen the app

If someone is having trouble joining your family group:

  1. Make sure you're sharing the correct 6-digit group code
  2. Check that the group hasn't reached its member limit (6 people)
  3. Verify everyone is using the latest version of MuteMerit
  4. Try generating a new invite code in Family Group settings
  5. Ensure all members have an internet connection when joining
