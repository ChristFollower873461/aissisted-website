KindnessQuest is designed for children ages 4 to 12:

  • Ages 4-6: Simple missions with parent guidance ("give a hug," "say thank you")

  • Ages 10-12: Complex missions encouraging leadership ("organize a food drive," "mentor a younger child")
💡 Parent Tip

Younger children may need help reading mission descriptions. Use this as bonding time — read together and discuss what kindness means!

Kids earn badges for different types of kindness achievements:

  • Category Badges: Complete multiple missions in a category (Family Helper, School Star, Nature Friend)
  • Streak Badges: Complete missions on consecutive days
  • Milestone Badges: Reach 10, 25, 50 completed missions
  • Special Badges: Complete secret surprise missions

All badges appear in the child's profile and can be viewed by parents in the Family Dashboard.

For Parents

Parent PIN & Family Dashboard

The Parent PIN is a 4-digit code that protects parent-only areas of the app:

  • Access the Family Dashboard to view all children's progress
  • Manage subscription settings
  • Adjust app preferences
  • View detailed activity reports
💡 Setting Up Your PIN

During your first app launch, you'll be prompted to create a Parent PIN. Choose something you'll remember but your kids won't guess!

The Family Dashboard gives parents complete visibility:

  • Activity Feed: See which missions each child completed and when
  • Progress Tracking: View character evolution stages for all children
  • Badge Collections: See all earned badges
  • Weekly Reports: Summaries of kindness activities
  • Conversation Starters: Suggested questions based on completed missions

All data is stored locally on your device — we don't upload your children's activity to any server.

If you forget your Parent PIN:

  1. On the PIN entry screen, tap "Forgot PIN?"
  2. You'll need to answer your security question
  3. After verification, you can create a new PIN
⚠️ Important

If you can't answer your security question, you'll need to reinstall the app. This will reset all data unless you have iCloud backup enabled.

Privacy & Safety

COPPA Compliance & Data Protection

Yes! KindnessQuest is fully compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

✓ No Accounts Required

Children use the app without creating any account or providing personal information.

✓ No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information about your children.

✓ Local Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to our servers.

✓ No Third-Party Sharing

We do not share any data with advertisers, analytics companies, or third parties.

✓ No Social Features

There are no chat features, friend lists, or ways for children to communicate with strangers.

KindnessQuest stores minimal data, all locally on your device:

  • Child Profile Names: Only you enter these — we don't upload them
  • Mission Completion Records: Stored only on your device
  • Badge Progress: Local game state only
  • App Preferences: Sound, notification, and display settings

Optional iCloud Backup: If you enable iCloud for KindnessQuest, your data is backed up to your personal iCloud account (encrypted and under your control).

You can delete data in several ways:

  1. Delete a Child Profile: Family Dashboard → Child Profile → Delete Profile
  2. Clear All Data: Settings → Privacy & Data → Clear All App Data
  3. Uninstall the App: Removes all local data (iCloud backup separate)

To delete iCloud backups:

  1. iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage
  2. Find KindnessQuest
  3. Delete Documents & Data

You can cancel your Premium subscription anytime through the App Store:

Troubleshooting

Common Issues & Solutions

If you're being asked for a PIN you didn't create:

  1. The PIN was likely set during initial setup
  2. Try common PINs you use (but remember this is meant to keep kids out!)
  3. If you can't remember, tap "Forgot PIN?" and answer your security question
  4. As a last resort, delete and reinstall the app (progress may be lost without iCloud)

If progress seems lost:

  1. Check that you're viewing the correct child profile
  2. Ensure you're signed in with the same Apple ID (if using iCloud)
  3. Check iCloud Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → ensure KindnessQuest is enabled
  4. Try pulling down on the Family Dashboard to refresh
💡 Prevention Tip

Enable iCloud backup to protect your children's progress! Go to Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → enable KindnessQuest.

If Premium content isn't accessible:

  1. Tap Settings → Restore Purchases
  2. Force-quit the app and reopen
  3. Check your subscription status in iPhone Settings → Subscriptions
  4. Ensure you're using the same Apple ID that purchased Premium

If issues persist, contact support with your proof of purchase.

KindnessQuest requires iOS 14.0 or later. For best performance:

  1. Update to the latest iOS version supported by your device
  2. Close other apps running in the background
  3. Free up storage space (Settings → General → iPhone Storage)
  4. Restart your device

If crashes continue, please contact support with your device model and iOS version.
