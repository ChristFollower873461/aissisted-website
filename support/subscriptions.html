Manage Your Subscriptions

Everything you need to know about managing, canceling, and restoring subscriptions for all AIssisted Consulting apps.

Step-by-Step Guides

How to Manage Subscriptions

1 Access Your Subscriptions

All subscriptions for AIssisted apps are managed through your Apple ID settings:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top of the screen (Apple ID)
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. You'll see a list of all your active and expired subscriptions
💡 Quick Access

You can also access subscriptions through the App Store: Open App Store → Tap your profile picture → Subscriptions.

2 Cancel a Subscription

To stop automatic renewal and cancel your subscription:

  1. In Settings → Subscriptions, tap the subscription you want to cancel
  2. Tap Cancel Subscription (or "Cancel Free Trial")
  3. Confirm your choice when prompted
⚠️ Important

You'll continue to have access to premium features until the end of your current billing period. Canceling stops the next renewal — it doesn't immediately end your access.

3 Restore Purchases

If you previously purchased a subscription and it's not showing:

  1. Open the app where you made the purchase
  2. Go to Settings or the Premium/Pro section
  3. Look for and tap Restore Purchases
  4. You may be prompted to sign in with your Apple ID
  5. Wait for the confirmation that purchases have been restored
💡 Troubleshooting

Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase. Family Sharing purchases must be restored by the family member who originally bought the subscription.

4 Request a Refund

Refund requests are handled by Apple, not AIssisted Consulting:

  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Tap "I'd like to" and choose "Request a refund"
  4. Select the reason for your refund request
  5. Choose the subscription or purchase you want refunded
  6. Submit your request
💡 Refund Tips

Apple typically reviews refund requests within 48 hours. Be specific about why you're requesting a refund. Refunds are granted on a case-by-case basis according to Apple's policies.
