Quick FAQs

Do I need to change my full tech stack first?

No. Most implementations start by improving the workflow around your current tools, then extending only where value is clear.

Can this work for local service businesses?

Yes. The industry pages in this candidate are built around HVAC, pest control, and plumbing because those teams need response speed and routing consistency.

How fast can we launch?

Typical first deployment is 2 to 4 weeks after scope approval, depending on workflow complexity and integration requirements.

What if fit is weak?

Discovery is designed to make that clear early. If practical fit is weak, we will say so and avoid forcing an implementation.
