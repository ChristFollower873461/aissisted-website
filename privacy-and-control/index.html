Privacy and control

Useful AI still needs clear boundaries.

The goal is practical support with human review, not handing sensitive details or important decisions to a tool without a plan.

Boundary check

Start by naming what should not move.

Some details need extra care. Some decisions should stay manual. Some work is not a good fit for AI support at all.

Review before tools

Privacy is part of the workflow, not a footnote.

Before adding pages, forms, or connected tools, the work needs visible limits. AIssisted Consulting keeps privacy, control, and human judgment in the conversation from the start.

What not to share

Passwords, private records, payment details, sensitive customer information, and other high-risk details need careful handling.

What needs review

Customer messages, owner decisions, family guidance, and operational changes should have a person in the loop.

What may stay manual

If a workflow depends on trust, safety, context, or judgment, support may be useful even when automation is not.

Plain limit

This is practical guidance, not a certification.

AIssisted Consulting does not claim legal, security, medical, financial, school-policy, child-safety, privacy, or compliance certification. Boundaries stay visible so the work stays practical and honest.
