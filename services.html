From voicemail to proposals, your dedicated AI agent handles the tasks that slow you down — so you can focus on growing your business. A complete AI receptionist for small business that works 24/7.

Voicemail Processing & Transcription

Every voicemail is transcribed within seconds, categorized by urgency, and delivered to you with context and recommended next steps. No more listening to messages or playing phone tag.

Lead Capture & Instant Response

New leads get an immediate, personalized response via text or email — 24/7. Your agent qualifies prospects, answers common questions, and books appointments while you sleep.

Email Inbox Monitoring

Your agent monitors designated inboxes, flags urgent messages, drafts replies for your approval, and auto-responds to routine inquiries. Stay on top of communication without the constant checking.

Proposal & Estimate Generation

Turn customer requests into professional proposals in minutes. Your agent pulls from your pricing templates, adds customer-specific details, and delivers polished estimates ready to send.

Wake up to a summary of yesterday's activity: new leads, appointments scheduled, follow-ups needed, and key metrics. Weekly deep-dives help you spot trends and optimize operations.

Appointment Scheduling

Your agent coordinates with customers to find available slots, sends calendar invites, handles rescheduling, and sends reminders — reducing no-shows and keeping your calendar full.

🔄

Customer Follow-up Sequences

Automated follow-ups that feel personal. From post-service check-ins to nurture campaigns for cold leads, your agent maintains relationships without you lifting a finger.

🌙

After-Hours Coverage

When your business closes, your AI employee keeps working. Emergency calls get routed appropriately. Routine inquiries get handled. You wake up to organized, actionable summaries.
