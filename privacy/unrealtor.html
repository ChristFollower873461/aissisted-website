UnRealtor privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

UnRealtor is published by AIssisted Consulting LLC.

Summary: UnRealtor is designed as a local-first real-estate deal coach. The app does not require an account, does not show ads, does not use advertising tracking SDKs, and does not sell imported report, property, or deal data.

Data The App Handles

UnRealtor may store the following information locally on your device:

  • Imported documents such as inspection reports, disclosures, contracts, or listing packets.
  • Camera scans created when you choose to scan paper documents.
  • Saved property records such as address, listing URL, seller type, contact path, notes, tour prep, and offer-readiness status.
  • Deal scenario inputs such as price, credits, loan type, occupancy, closing costs, and hold period.
  • App preferences such as onboarding completion.

This information is processed on-device so the app can classify reports, run negotiation math, prepare property actions, and restore your last state.

Data Collection And Sharing

AIssisted Consulting LLC does not collect imported reports, camera scans, saved property notes, extracted report text, deal scenarios, or app preferences from the app and does not transmit that information to company servers.

If the app uses subscription billing, the app and its billing partners may process purchase-related data needed to offer, validate, restore, and manage the subscription. That purchase flow is handled through the platform store and RevenueCat.

What UnRealtor Does Not Do

  • UnRealtor does not create user accounts.
  • UnRealtor does not send advertising analytics events.
  • UnRealtor does not use advertising SDKs.
  • UnRealtor does not share app data with data brokers or marketing partners.
  • UnRealtor does not sell personal information.

Data Retention And Deletion

Imported reports, saved properties, and app state remain on the device until you remove them.

You can delete locally stored data by deleting imported reports inside the app, removing saved property records, clearing the app's storage in Android settings, or uninstalling the app.

Third-Party Services

The app relies on standard platform services needed to install, run, and subscribe through Apple App Store, Google Play, Expo Application Services, RevenueCat, and the mobile operating system.

These services may process limited purchase or device-related information required for subscription status, purchase restoration, crash handling, app distribution, or app installation. Imported documents, camera scans, saved property notes, extracted report text, and deal scenario content are intended to remain on-device and are not intentionally transmitted to AIssisted Consulting LLC servers.

Children

UnRealtor is intended for adults dealing with home-purchase, home-sale, and negotiation decisions. It is not directed to children.

Future Cloud Sync

UnRealtor is local-first today. If optional cloud sync is added later, this policy, store privacy disclosures, and in-app copy will be updated before that feature is enabled in production.

Contact

If you have privacy questions, contact AIssisted Consulting LLC through aissistedconsulting.com/contact.
