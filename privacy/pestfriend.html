Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 15, 2026

PestFriend (Bundle ID: com.aissisted.pestfriend)
Developed by AIssisted Consulting LLC

Introduction

AIssisted Consulting LLC ("we," "our," or "us") takes your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how PestFriend handles your data, including photos you take for pest identification and AI chat interactions. We believe in transparency about our AI-powered features.

Key Points: Your chat history stays on your device. Photos you take are sent to Anthropic's AI for identification but are not stored or used to train AI models. No user accounts required.

Information We Collect

Pest Identification Photos

When you use the pest identification feature:

  • What you submit: Photos you take or select from your camera roll of pests, insects, or pest damage
  • How it's processed: Images are sent to Anthropic's Claude AI API for analysis and identification
  • What Anthropic receives: Only the image itself, with no identifying information about you
  • Image retention: Anthropic does not retain images after processing; they are processed in real-time and discarded
  • Your control: You choose which photos to submit; camera access requires your permission
Important: Do not submit photos containing people, faces, license plates, or other sensitive personal information. Only submit photos of pests, insects, and pest-related damage.

Location Data (Optional)

Location access is optional and used only for regional pest information:

  • Purpose: To provide pest information relevant to your geographic region (e.g., common pests in your area)
  • How it's used: General region identification only; precise coordinates are not stored
  • Your control: Completely optional; you can deny access and still use all app features
  • Retention: Location data is processed in real-time and not retained

AI Chat History

Your conversations with the AI pest expert:

  • Storage: Chat history is stored locally on your device only
  • No server storage: We do not store your chat history on our servers
  • AI processing: Individual messages are sent to Anthropic's API for response generation but are not retained by Anthropic
  • Your control: You can clear chat history at any time in app settings
  • Privacy: Anthropic does not use your conversations to train their AI models

App Preferences

Stored locally on your device:

  • Your saved pest identifications and notes
  • Bookmarked pest guides
  • App settings (notifications, theme, etc.)
  • Search history within the app

Subscription Information

Payment processing is handled by Apple and RevenueCat:

  • We do not collect or store payment information
  • We receive only anonymized subscription status (active/inactive)
  • All transactions are processed through Apple's secure payment system

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or phone number (no account required)
  • Contacts from your device
  • Access to your full photo library (only photos you explicitly select)
  • Browsing history outside the app
  • Health or fitness data
  • Precise location history
  • Device identifiers linked to your identity

AI Processing & Third-Party Services

Anthropic API

We use Anthropic's Claude AI for pest identification and chat features:

  • Photos and chat messages are sent to Anthropic's API
  • Anthropic processes this data according to their Commercial Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic does not use your data to train their AI models
  • Data is transmitted securely using TLS encryption
  • Images and messages are not retained after processing

Other Service Providers

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (see RevenueCat Privacy Policy)
  • Apple App Store: App distribution and in-app purchases

  • All personal data (chat history, saved pests, notes) is stored locally on your device

  • We do not maintain servers that store your pest photos or chat history
  • iCloud backup may include your local app data if enabled on your device

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

PestFriend is intended for general audiences and complies with COPPA:

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No accounts or contact information required
  • Photos submitted for identification should only contain pests/insects, not people
  • Parents: If you believe your child has submitted personal information, contact us at pj@aissistedconsulting.com for deletion

  • Right to access your data

  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing

Your Choices

  • Camera Access: Disable in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Camera → PestFriend
  • Location Access: Disable in iOS Settings → Privacy & Security → Location Services → PestFriend
  • Clear Chat History: Use the "Clear History" option in app settings
  • Delete All Data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data

Data Retention

  • Local app data: Retained until you delete the app or clear data manually
  • Images sent to AI: Not retained by Anthropic after processing
  • Subscription data: Anonymous status retained for 12 months

International Data Transfers

Anthropic's API may process data in the United States. By using the AI features, you consent to this transfer. Anthropic maintains appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.
