Privacy Policy

Wanderlux (Bundle ID: com.aissisted.haven)

Introduction

AIssisted Consulting LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Wanderlux app. We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly what data we collect and why.

Quick Summary: We collect minimal data necessary to provide location-based resort recommendations. Your location data stays on your device unless you explicitly grant permission. We don't sell your data, and we don't require user accounts.

Information We Collect

Location Data

Wanderlux requests access to your device's location to show nearby luxury resorts. This is entirely optional:

  • Precise Location: With your permission, we access your GPS coordinates to find resorts near you
  • How it's used: Solely to sort and display nearby resorts; we do not store your location history
  • Your control: You can deny location access and manually search for any destination
  • Data retention: Location queries are processed in real-time and not retained on our servers

Search Preferences & Bookmarks

Stored locally on your device:

  • Resort bookmarks and favorites
  • Recent searches
  • Filter preferences (price range, amenities, ratings)
  • Viewed resort history

This data never leaves your device unless you enable iCloud backup.

Analytics Data

We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage analytics to improve the app:

  • Feature usage (e.g., which filters are popular)
  • App performance metrics
  • Crash and error data (via Sentry)
  • No personally identifiable information is collected
  • IP addresses are not logged

Subscription Information

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple through RevenueCat:

  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • RevenueCat provides anonymized subscription status (active/expired)
  • Transaction history remains with Apple

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or phone number (no account required)
  • Contacts from your device
  • Photos or camera access
  • Health or fitness data
  • Browsing history outside the app
  • Device identifiers linked to your identity

How We Use Your Information

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis

Third-Party Services

Affiliate Partners

Wanderlux contains affiliate links to booking platforms including:

  • Booking.com
  • Hotels.com
  • Expedia
  • Other travel booking sites

When you tap to book, you leave our app and are subject to those platforms' privacy policies. We do not receive information about your bookings.

Service Providers

  • RevenueCat: Subscription management (see RevenueCat Privacy Policy)
  • PostHog: Anonymous analytics (see PostHog Privacy Policy)
  • Sentry: Error tracking (see Sentry Privacy Policy)

Data Storage & Security

  • Personal preferences and bookmarks are stored locally on your device using iOS secure storage
  • Analytics data is stored securely by PostHog with encryption at rest
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect any data we process
  • We do not maintain servers that store your personal data

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Wanderlux is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately at pj@aissistedconsulting.com and we will delete such information.

Your Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected about you
  • Know whether your personal information is sold or disclosed (we do not sell data)
  • Say no to the sale of personal information (N/A as we don't sell data)
  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Equal service and price (we do not discriminate)

To exercise these rights, contact us at pj@aissistedconsulting.com.

European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate data
  • Request erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time

Since we don't collect email or create user accounts, most requests will involve clearing app data from your device. To make a formal request, email pj@aissistedconsulting.com.

Your Choices

  • Location: Disable in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Wanderlux
  • Analytics: No opt-out required—data is anonymous and cannot identify you
  • Delete Data: Uninstall the app to remove all local data, or contact us to request deletion of any server-stored analytics

Data Retention

  • Local data: Retained until you delete the app or clear data
  • Analytics: Anonymous data retained for 12 months, then aggregated or deleted
  • Crash logs: Retained for 90 days

International Data Transfers

Analytics data may be processed on servers located in the United States. By using Wanderlux, you consent to this transfer. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for any international data transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the effective date. Your continued use of Wanderlux after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
