Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 19, 2026

Wanderlux (Bundle ID: com.aissisted.haven)
Developed by AIssisted Consulting LLC

Introduction

AIssisted Consulting LLC ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This policy describes the current free version of Wanderlux (version 1.2.0 and later unless this page is updated).

Quick Summary: Wanderlux does not require an account and does not collect data from the app. Routes, searches, trips, favorites, settings, and optional location calculations stay on your device. We do not track you, sell data, or serve ads.

Data We Do Not Collect

The current production configuration does not send personal data, device identifiers, usage events, location, diagnostics, or crash reports to AIssisted Consulting or to an analytics, subscription, or error-tracking service. Specifically:

  • No name, email address, phone number, or user account
  • No advertising or cross-app tracking
  • No analytics events or device identifiers
  • No developer-collected crash or performance reports
  • No payment or subscription information; the current version offers no purchases
  • No contacts, photos, camera, microphone, health, or financial data

Information Processed on Your Device

Optional Location

If you choose “near me,” Wanderlux asks iOS for location permission. Your coordinates are used on the device to calculate distances to the app's bundled resort catalog. Wanderlux does not transmit or retain your location on our servers, and you can deny or revoke permission in iOS Settings.

Trips, Searches, Favorites, and Settings

The following information is stored locally so the app can remember your planning work:

  • Saved trips and overnight stops
  • Favorite properties and Wanderlist selections
  • Route inputs, travel-party details, dates, and family preferences
  • Search filters and appearance settings

This local app data is not available to us. Your device backup settings are controlled by Apple and may preserve app data in a device or iCloud backup under Apple's terms.

External Property and Maps Links

At your request, Wanderlux can open a property website or a directions link in Apple Maps or another maps service. That action leaves Wanderlux and is governed by the receiving service's privacy policy. The app does not receive information about what you do after the external service opens.

Inactive Service Integrations

The app's codebase can support PostHog, Sentry, and RevenueCat in a separately configured future release. Those services are disabled in the current production version because no production keys are configured. No events, crash reports, device identifiers, or subscription status are sent to them by this version.

Data Storage & Security

  • Planning data and preferences are stored locally in the app's iOS storage
  • Wanderlux does not maintain a user account database or a server containing your app data
  • Delete the app to remove its locally stored data from the device; device backups remain subject to your Apple backup settings

Children's Privacy

Wanderlux is a general-audience travel-planning app and does not collect personal information from users, including children. It has no social features, messaging, user-generated content, or advertising.

Your Privacy Rights

Because we do not receive your Wanderlux app data, we generally have no user record to access, correct, export, or delete. You remain in control of the information stored on your device:

  • Location: Disable in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Location Services > Wanderlux
  • Local app data: Delete Wanderlux from your device
  • Backups: Manage device and iCloud backups through Apple

Questions or rights requests can be sent to pj@aissistedconsulting.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the effective date. Your continued use of Wanderlux after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
