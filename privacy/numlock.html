Privacy Policy

NumLock (Bundle ID: com.aissisted.numlock)

Introduction

AIssisted Consulting LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for NumLock, a number puzzle game. We collect minimal data and prioritize your privacy.

Privacy-First Design: NumLock stores all game data locally on your device. We don't require an account, don't track your location, and don't collect personally identifiable information. Your game is yours alone.

Information We Collect

Game Data (Local Only)

All game data is stored exclusively on your device using iOS secure storage:

  • Game scores and high scores
  • Number of games played
  • Game statistics (best times, average scores, streaks)
  • Local leaderboard entries
  • Game preferences (sound settings, theme preferences, difficulty)
  • In-progress game state (for resume functionality)

This data never leaves your device unless you have iCloud backup enabled for the app.

Analytics Data

We collect minimal, anonymous usage data through RevenueCat (for subscription analytics only):

  • Subscription status (active/inactive)
  • Anonymous purchase events
  • No gameplay data is collected
  • No device identifiers linked to your identity

Subscription Information

All payment processing is handled by Apple:

  • We do not collect or store your payment information
  • Apple processes all transactions
  • We receive only anonymized subscription status from RevenueCat

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • Name, email address, or any contact information
  • Location data (GPS is never accessed)
  • Photos, camera, or microphone access
  • Contacts from your device
  • Device identifiers that could track you across apps
  • Browsing history or usage outside the app
  • Advertising identifiers (IDFA)

How We Use Information

Data Type Purpose Storage Location
Game Scores/Stats Track personal progress, populate local leaderboard Your device only
Preferences Remember your settings Your device only
Subscription Status Enable premium features Anonymized via RevenueCat

Local Leaderboard

NumLock includes a local leaderboard feature:

  • Scores are stored only on your device
  • No online multiplayer or global leaderboards
  • No sharing of scores with other users
  • You can reset your leaderboard at any time in app settings

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Apple App Store: For app distribution and in-app purchases (see Apple Privacy Policy)
  • RevenueCat: For subscription management (see RevenueCat Privacy Policy)

Data Storage & Security

  • All game data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and secure storage
  • We do not operate servers that store your game data
  • If you enable iCloud backup, your game data may be included in your iCloud backup at Apple's discretion
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all local game data unless backed up to iCloud

Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

NumLock is suitable for all ages and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13
  • No data is shared with third parties for advertising
  • No behavioral advertising is used
  • No links to external websites (except this privacy policy)
  • Parents can contact us with any concerns at pj@aissistedconsulting.com

Your Privacy Rights

California Residents (CCPA)

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

  • We do not collect personal information that identifies you
  • We do not sell any data (we have no data to sell)
  • You can request information about any data we may have by emailing pj@aissistedconsulting.com

European Users (GDPR)

For users in the European Economic Area:

  • Since we don't collect personal data or create accounts, most GDPR rights are exercised by simply using or deleting the app
  • To "delete" your data: uninstall the app or reset from settings
  • To make a formal request, contact pj@aissistedconsulting.com

Your Choices

  • Clear Game Data: Go to Settings → Reset Progress within the app
  • Delete All Data: Uninstall the app from your device
  • iCloud Backup: Control in iOS Settings → [Your Name] → iCloud → Manage Storage

Data Retention

  • Game data: Retained on your device until you delete the app or clear data
  • Analytics: Anonymous subscription events retained for 12 months

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes will be posted here with an updated effective date. Continued use of NumLock after changes constitutes acceptance.
