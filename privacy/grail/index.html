Introduction

AIssisted Consulting LLC ("we," "our," or "us") provides Grail to help businesses prepare, review, and operate source-backed marketing workflows. This policy explains what information Grail may process, how it is used, and the choices available to you.

Key points: We do not sell personal information. The submitted iOS build saves campaign drafts, proof packets, owner-review settings, and recent activity on the device. Connected workspace or onboarding services may process business details only as needed to create, support, and secure the service.

Information We Process

Campaign and workspace details

Grail may process business names, offers, service areas, target markets, approved proof facts, public source URLs, calls to action, selected channels, draft copy, proof packets, and owner-review status.

Fit Check and onboarding details

If you use a Grail onboarding or Fit Check service, you may provide business and contact details, current marketing channels, proof assets, and workflow preferences.

Support communications

If you contact us, we may process the contact information and issue details you provide. Do not include private customer information unless it is necessary and approved for support.

How We Use Information

  • To build, save, and maintain your Grail workspace
  • To generate, edit, proof-check, copy, and review marketing drafts
  • To respond to support and workspace requests
  • To secure, debug, and improve the app and related services
  • To comply with legal, store, security, and business obligations

Current iOS App Behavior

The submitted iOS workspace stores its working data locally on the device. It does not publish or schedule posts, send messages, spend advertising money, or connect customer channels by itself. Opening a public source URL leaves Grail and uses the device's normal web browser.

Information We Do Not Sell

We do not sell personal information. We do not use private business details to train public AI models. We do not intentionally publish private materials unless the business has approved the relevant content, source, and channel through a separately authorized service.

Data Security

Your Choices

  • You choose what business information and approved proof you enter.
  • Removing the iOS app removes its locally stored workspace data.
  • You may contact us to request correction, deletion, or export of information held by a connected Grail service.
