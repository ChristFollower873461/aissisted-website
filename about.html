Meet PJ Standley — Based in Ocala, FL

I'm an engineer with a BSET degree and over 20 years of experience building and maintaining precision test environments. That background in reliable systems and mission-critical infrastructure directly shaped how I approach AI — it has to work, period. I'm proud to be locally owned and operated in Ocala, Florida.

I also own and operate Standley Pest Control, a small business serving residential and commercial customers throughout Central Florida and North Central Florida. I know firsthand what it's like to miss calls while you're on a job, to lose evenings catching up on admin work, and to wonder how many leads slipped through the cracks.

I built AIssisted Consulting because I needed a solution that didn't exist: a dedicated AI agent that runs on my own hardware, keeps my data private, and actually understands my business. Not a chatbot. Not a shared SaaS product. A real AI employee. Now I help other small businesses in the Central Florida community work smarter and capture every opportunity.
