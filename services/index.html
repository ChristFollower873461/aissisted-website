Services

Practical AI help for the work already on your plate.

AIssisted Consulting helps identify, configure, and review AI-supported workflows for small service businesses without promising full automation or replacing human judgment.

Map the first fix See the starting method

Fit

Good service work is specific.

The best starting point is a concrete workflow: a missed call pattern, a handoff, a repeat follow-up, or an owner report that is too hard to see today.

Core service areas

Start where support can be useful and reviewable.

Intake review

Clarify what comes in, what matters, what can be summarized, and where a person needs to decide.

Scheduling support

Shape reminders, handoffs, and update points around the way the business already works.

Follow-up systems

Make customer follow-up more visible and consistent without pretending relationships can run unattended.

Owner visibility

Turn daily activity into summaries or review views that help the owner know what needs attention.

Wrong fit

Not every workflow should be automated.

AIssisted Consulting should not be used to bypass privacy, professional judgment, safety decisions, or customer trust. The service starts with a practical review of what should be supported, what should stay manual, and what needs human review.
