What's Happening

Free AI Has No Boundaries

When a child opens ChatGPT, there are no age-appropriate filters, no parent visibility, and no limits on what they can ask or what the AI will say. The AI doesn't know it's talking to a 10-year-old. It doesn't care. It will answer questions about violence, generate inappropriate content, and give advice no child should receive — all while harvesting every word your child types to train its next model. This isn't hypothetical. It's happening right now in millions of homes.
